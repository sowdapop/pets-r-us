Meet our trainer, Olivia!

Olivia grew up with her family in Ohio breeding and training dogs for hunting and protection. This gave Olivia experience with training dogs of all ages of various breeds, which makes her the perfect asset to our Pets-R-Us team. With this knowledge, Olivia has helped thousands of dogs overcome fears and undesirable behaviors that kept them from being adopted. Now, she proudly offers her services to our customers.

Schedule today if your puppy needs to learn the ropes, but you're not sure how to show him, if your adolescent dog is acting out, or if your old dog needs to learn a few new tricks. We offer schedules and prices to suit your needs. If you are looking for specialized hunting or protection trainer, speak with Olivia directly.

The General Obedience Program teaches proper leash behavior, simple tricks like "sit" and "stay," and how to use (and enforce) the word NO!

The Potty Training Program teaches methods that work and explains WHY!

The Bad Dogs Wanted gets to the root of the pooch's problem and helps you work through the resolution for a win/win.

The Obedience Refresher and Old Bad Dogs Wanted programs are the same, but with the mature dog in mind.
